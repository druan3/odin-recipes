Chicken Alfredo

Description

A quick and easy chicken alfredo dinner with broccoli, zucchini, and red bell pepper. The sauce is enriched by cream cheese.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces dry fettucine pasta
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cooked and cubed
  • 2 cups chopped fresh broccoli
  • 2 small zucchini, julienned
  • 1/2 cup chopped red bell pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add pasta, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
  3. While pasta is cooking, melt cream cheese and butter in a skillet over low heat.
  4. Stir until smooth. Stir in milk, and season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer for 3 minutes, or until thickened, stirring constantly.
  6. Mix in chicken, broccoli, zucchini, and red pepper.
  7. Cook 3 minutes over medium heat, then reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
  8. Serve over fettucine.
