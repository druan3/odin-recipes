Clam Chowder

Description

This clam chowder is a hearty recipe that will warm you up on cold winter days.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped onion
  • 4 cups peeled and cubed potatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 cups half-and-half
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 (10 ounce) cans minced clams

Steps

  1. Place diced bacon in large stockpot over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir until almost crisp.
  3. Add onion; cook and stir until tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in potatoes and add water; season with salt and pepper and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer, uncovered, until potatoes are tender, about 15 minutes.
  6. Pour in half-and-half and add butter.
  7. Drain clams, reserving clam liquid; stir clams and 1/2 of the clam liquid into the soup.
  8. Cook until heated through, without boiling, about 5 minutes.
